Mammoth Cheese Bowl

Description

Mammoth Cheese Bowl is a food item in The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim. When eaten, it restores 10 points of health and 15 points of stamina. It is found in giant camps, and is harvestable from large hide sacks of mammoth cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bowl
  • Fermented Mammoth Milk

Steps

  1. Milk the mammoth
  2. Let the milk ferment
  3. Once fermented, stir into a bowl
